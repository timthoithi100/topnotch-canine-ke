Meet the Director

Bonifance Mokua — Director | Head of Training

Bonifance Mokua is a retired Military Police veteran certified IED/EDD Trainer with deep experience training Kenyan military K9 Units and serving under UNMAS in UN Somalia. He led a team of six trainers mentoring Somali Police Forces on CIED and EDD handling.

He was in charge of K9 Security in cargo and airplane screening at Adan Abdulle International Airport in Mogadishu as well as the Mogadishu Sea port. He founded Top Notch Canine Capability in 2016 with a single mission: to deliver security beyond human limitations.

Associate Member of NASDU (National Association of Security Dog Users), PROSAK (Protective and Safety Association of Kenya), and EAKC (East Africa Kennel Club).

Who We Are

Kenya's Premier K9 Security Provider

TOPNOTCH K-9 CAPABILITY is a premier provider of highly trained Security Working Dogs — Patrol & Guarding, Detection, Tracking and SAR — working directly with members of security forces as well as other elite military organizations across Kenya and Africa with high-level threat protection.

TOPNOTCH K-9 is able to provide comprehensive security solutions tailored to our clients' needs. Our team is comprised of active and retired members of specialized law enforcement units across the country as well as other elite military organizations. Our clients have immediate access to individuals with extensive and unrivaled experience in all areas of Security.
