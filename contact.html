Career Opportunities

We are always looking for exceptional K9 handlers, trainers and support staff who share our commitment to security excellence.

K9 Handler

Full-Time

Operate and care for a certified working dog across patrol, detection or VIP protection deployments. Reporting to the Head of Training.

Requirements

Valid NASDU K9 handler certification
2+ years hands-on K9 handling experience
Physical fitness and willingness to work shifts
Clean background check

Dog Trainer

Full-Time

Design and implement training programs for new K9 recruits and refresher certification for existing operational dogs.

Requirements

NASDU trainer-level certification preferred
3+ years training working dogs (patrol/detection)
Proven track record in obedience and scent work
Strong record-keeping and reporting skills
Apply Now

Kennel Technician

Full-Time

Responsible for the daily care, health, nutrition and welfare of our K9 fleet. Coordinate with the veterinary team on health protocols.

Requirements

Certificate in Animal Health or equivalent
1+ year kennel or veterinary clinic experience
Comfortable handling large, working breeds
