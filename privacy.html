Privacy Policy

How Top Notch Canine Capability collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Effective: 1 January 2026
Last updated: May 2026

1. Who We Are

Top Notch Canine Capability ("we", "us", "our") is a K9 security and dog training company registered in Kenya, operating from Balozi Road, Bishop Court, Kitengela. We are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information only when you voluntarily provide it to us through our contact form, by calling us, or by emailing or messaging us directly. This may include:

  • Your name and the name of your company or organisation
  • Your email address and phone number
  • Your site location or address relevant to the service you are enquiring about
  • Details of your security requirements as provided in your message

We do not collect any data automatically through cookies, tracking pixels or analytics scripts on this website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and provide the services you have requested
  • Prepare quotations and consultation plans
  • Communicate with you about ongoing deployments or service arrangements

We do not use your personal information for any marketing, profiling or automated decision-making purposes.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your details internally with members of our team who need them to respond to your enquiry or deliver services. We will only share information with external parties if required to do so by Kenyan law.

5. How We Store and Protect Your Data

Enquiry data received by email or WhatsApp is stored on our secure business devices and email systems. We take reasonable steps to ensure this information is protected against unauthorised access, loss or disclosure. Data is retained only for as long as it is relevant to your ongoing or potential service relationship with us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information, subject to any legal obligations we may have to retain it

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at clientservice@topnotchk9capability.co.ke.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version will always be available on this page. Continued use of our website or services after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Address: Balozi Road, Bishop Court, Kitengela, Kenya
